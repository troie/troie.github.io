why we do what we do

Inspired by rapid industrialization and economic growth in the latter half of 20th century, vast majority of the organizations across Taiwan adopted manufacturing way of things. This success has since become a serious bottleneck for Taiwan to continue being competitive in today’s fast changing markets.

We believe user centered mindset and process combined with solid manufacturing abilities can help our clients innovate and create new values on a global level. We also believe it is only sustainable when the mindset and process can be fully internalized by our clients. To achieve this feat we have to dismantle traditional consulting model and use our radical collaboration approach to tackle business challenges together.

We are Pebbo and our expedition continues.

二十世紀中後，台灣因高速工業化帶來奇蹟似的經濟成長，其它產業順理成章地承襲了製造業的思維及模式。上個世紀的勝利方程式，卻成了今天無法創新的瓶頸。我們需要提供一個不同的觀點，啟發企業重新檢視創新，持續成長。

我們相信以人為本的思維結合台灣企業深厚的製造底子，能為這個世界創造全新的價值。也唯有扎實地提升企業內部創新能力，才能在瞬息萬變的市場上持續競爭。因此，我們捨棄了傳統的顧問模式，與客戶組成專案團隊，透過反覆實作與經驗共享，解決企業難題。

改變不易。猶如探險，讓我們一起前進。
