Innovation Leadership Development

創新能力開發

Our vision is to help businesses build innovation skills that enable employees to become expert problem solvers. To ensure mastery and quality of work, we act as coaches to other team members throughout the expedition. Employees can have difficulty adjusting their mentality to a user-centered approach. This continuous leadership development assists them in realizing the value of listening to user feedback. They can then apply this mindset to their daily work and even lead teams in future innovation efforts. The majority of our clients keep their original team. Some have even expanded the team and strengthened innovation culture within the company.
