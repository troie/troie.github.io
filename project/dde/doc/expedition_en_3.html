Analogous Research

異業類比研究

We often find inspiration for solutions outside the typical areas of research. We believe insights from unrelated fields can be cross-pollinated. When we worked with Asia Miles to increase the number of memberships for their royalty program, we were inspired by the attention grabbing abilities of street vendors. Mr. Lu, a successful vendor with several booths all over Hong Kong, gave us insightful tips on sales strategies. His input helped us create a brand new membership recruitment process. Within two years of the launch, Asia Miles’ membership increased by 250%.
