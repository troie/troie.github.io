User Need & Design Trend Research

用戶需求與設計趨勢研究

User research has been the talk of the industry in recent years. However, in addition to user research, we find it also important to look at the problem from a wider perspective and understand the market and trend. We call this design research. Imagine a goat being chased by a lion on a vast grasslands. The role of a user researcher is to observe the scene up close from behind a rock while a designer researcher stands on a hill trying to discern the balance in the food chain.

Our project team is made up of both user and design researchers. Throughout the expedition, their most important role is to validate user needs and match market demands.
