During our work with clients, we have accumulated observations on the roadblocks to innovation frequently faced by companies. We consolidated our findings and shared these insights with clients in innovation seminars. The topics include reasons why large corporations are unable to innovate, how to start from 0, and more. To date, we have given more than 20 seminars to our clients and the general public. These events take places at Yahoo!, E Sun Bank, designers week, etc. We have also been covered by Common Wealth magazine, EMBA magazine, and Manager Today to share our experiences and insights on innovation.
